Our Process

100% Export-Grade A+ Quality

1️⃣ Sustainable Sourcing

We operate a transparent "Farm-to-Gate" model, partnering directly with Tanzanian smallholder farmers.

2️⃣ Harvest & Primary Drying

Harvested at peak potency, our chili is sun-dried in the natural Tanzanian heat.

3️⃣ Precision Re-Drying

To ensure shelf stability and premium quality, spices are re-dried at our processing site to achieve a moisture content of below 10%.

4️⃣ Multi-Stage Sorting & QC

Every batch undergoes rigorous sorting and quality control (QC) to remove impurities and select only the best grades.

5️⃣ Expert Packaging

Final products are packed in moisture-resistant materials.

6️⃣ Container Loading

Final produce is securely loaded into containers at our Morogoro hub for international delivery to global markets.
